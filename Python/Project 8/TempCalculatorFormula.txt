1. Celsius to Fahrenheit (CtoF)
> F = (9/5)*C + 32
2. Celsius to Kelvin (CtoK)
> K = C + 273.15
3. Fahrenheit to Celsius (FtoC)
> C = (5/9)*(F - 32)
4. Fahrenheit to Kelvin (FtoK)
> K = (5/9)*(F - 32) + 273.15
5. Kelvin to Celsius (KtoC)
> C = K - 273.15
6. Kelvin to Fahrenheit (KtoF)
> F = (9/5)*K - 459.67
